Which two websites are you inspired by?

I was inspired by livwrk.com and workstead.com

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size, or balance?

  • Workstead's pages are clean, simple, and well-balanced. Each page has a block that serves as a menu and provides textual information while the surrounding page provides a background that is illustrative of their work. The site is generally easy to navigate.
  • Livwrk's homepage is straightforward and divided into 3 horizontal sections: the name, a mission statement, and a menu. Each page is well-balanced, with 2 or fewer columns, and with white space that accentuates the positive space.

What makes them similar and different?

Workspace relies largely on the background to provide a relief for the text, while livwrk generally provides a more traditional 2-column layout for information. Neither website overwhelms with information; instead each provides easy access points for describing the company itself and leading to ways to utilize the company's resourses.
